# Jigglr

**Keep your Mac awake, your way.**

Jigglr is a tiny macOS menu bar app that gently keeps your Mac awake and
active — no more sleep, screensavers, or "away" status during long reads,
presentations, downloads, or remote sessions. It lives quietly in your menu
bar, uses almost no resources, and is private by design: no accounts, no
tracking, and nothing ever leaves your Mac.

## Features

- **One-tap control** — start or stop from the menu bar, or with the global
 shortcut ⌘⌃M.
- **Adjustable timing** — choose how long to wait before starting and how
 often to move.
- **Gentle, natural motion** — subtle movement that keeps your Mac awake
 without getting in your way.
- **Private by design** — no accounts, no analytics, no network access.
- **Lightweight** — lives in the menu bar with no Dock clutter.
- **Launch at Login** — set it once and forget it.

## Jigglr PRO

A one-time unlock (no subscriptions) adds:

- **Smart Motion** — natural, varied cursor movement.
- **Silent Mode** — stay awake without visibly moving the pointer.
- **Automatic clicks** — left, right, or double, at a fixed position if you like.
- **Auto-stop** — end on a timer or when the battery runs low.
- **Custom menu bar icons** — make it yours.

## Availability

Jigglr is available on the **Mac App Store** for macOS 14 and later.

## Privacy

Jigglr collects no data. Everything runs entirely on your Mac. See the
privacy policy for details.
