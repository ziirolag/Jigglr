Purchase & support

Questions about upgrading? Here’s everything you need.

How do I upgrade to PRO?
Open the Jigglr menu and click Upgrade to PRO. The purchase is handled securely by the App Store — no account or sign-up needed.
I bought PRO but it’s not showing up.
Open the upgrade window and click Restore Purchase. Make sure you’re signed in to the App Store with the same Apple ID you used to buy it.
Is it a subscription?
No. PRO is a one-time purchase. Buy it once and it stays unlocked — there’s nothing to renew.
