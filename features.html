Jigglr Features

Built for focus, privacy, and quick control.

Everything included

Menu bar first

Everything is one click away without interrupting your workflow.

Start anywhere

Press ⌘⌃M (Command + Control + M) anywhere to start or stop Jigglr without opening the menu.

Private by default

No analytics, no tracking, no accounts. Jigglr runs entirely on your Mac.

Smart Motion

Natural movement patterns help your Mac stay awake with less obvious motion.

Battery-aware

Auto-stop on low battery when you need to preserve charge on the go.

Fast setup

Enable accessibility once, pick your interval, and go.

Getting started

  1. Click the Jigglr icon in your menu bar.
  2. Choose how often it should move the cursor.
  3. Press Start — or use the shortcut ⌘⌃M to toggle it anytime.
