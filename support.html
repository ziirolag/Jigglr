Keep your Mac awake. Simple, private, and out of your way.

Need help with Jigglr? Most questions are answered below. If you can't find what you need, email jigglr@goatmail.uk and we'll get back to you.

Getting started

  1. Click the Jigglr icon in your menu bar.
  2. Choose how often it should signal activity to keep your Mac awake.
  3. Press Start — or use the shortcut ⌘⌃M to toggle it anytime.

Frequently asked questions

Does Jigglr need any special permissions?

No. Jigglr keeps your Mac awake using the system’s built-in power management. It never moves your cursor or clicks, so there’s nothing to grant — just open the menu and press Start.

How do I keep Jigglr running when I log in?

Open the menu and enable Launch at Login. macOS will start Jigglr automatically each time you sign in.

What's the keyboard shortcut?

Press ⌘⌃M (Command + Control + M) anywhere to start or stop Jigglr without opening the menu.

Does Jigglr keep working when my Mac is locked?

Yes. Locking your screen doesn’t stop Jigglr — it keeps your Mac awake (and, with PRO, your display) even at the lock screen. It only stops when you press Stop, an auto-stop rule triggers, or you quit the app.

Does Jigglr keep my Teams or Slack status green?

No. Jigglr keeps your Mac awake but never simulates keyboard or mouse input, so chat apps like Microsoft Teams and Slack may still show you as “away” after their idle timeout. Jigglr is built to prevent sleep — not to fake activity.

What's included in PRO?

  • Keep display awake — keep the screen lit too, not just the Mac
  • Auto-stop on a timer or low battery
  • Custom menu bar icons

How do I upgrade to PRO?

Open the menu and click Upgrade to PRO. The purchase is handled securely by the App Store.

I bought PRO but it's not showing up.

Open the upgrade window and click Restore Purchase. Make sure you're signed in to the App Store with the same Apple ID you used to buy it.

Does Jigglr collect my data?

No. Jigglr runs entirely on your Mac and does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal information. See our Privacy Policy for details.
